Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At Cyber-Math Overdrive ("we", "our", or "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our website and web application located at cybermathgame.xyz.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Because this tool is utilized by students and educators, privacy is our primary focus. We do not collect, ask for, or process any Personally Identifiable Information (PII). We do not collect names, email addresses, physical addresses, or phone numbers.

2. Information We Do Collect (Anonymized Game Data)

To allow users to save their progress, our system utilizes Google Firebase. When you create a "New Hacker," the system generates a random 6-character alphanumeric code (e.g., X7K9P2). Associated with this random code, we store:

  • Current Level / Node reached
  • Current Game Score and Top Score
  • In-game currency (Data Cores) balance
  • Unlocked cosmetic themes and upgrades

This data is completely anonymized and cannot be traced back to a specific individual.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We use your browser's Local Storage to securely remember your 6-character Hacker ID on your specific device. This allows the game to automatically log you back in upon your return without requiring you to memorize the code. You can clear this at any time by utilizing the "Logout" feature in the game.

4. Third-Party Advertising

We may use third-party advertising companies, such as Google AdSense, to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

5. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can remove such data.
